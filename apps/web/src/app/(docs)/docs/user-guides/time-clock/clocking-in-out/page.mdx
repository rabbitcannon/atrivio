# Clocking In & Out

Learn how to use the time clock to track your work hours.

## Overview

The time clock allows staff members to clock in and out of their shifts. All time entries are tracked and can be reviewed by managers for payroll processing.

## Accessing the Time Clock

### Staff Portal (Mobile-Friendly)

Access the dedicated time clock at:

```
https://app.atrivio.com/{your-org-slug}/time
```

This URL works great on mobile devices and provides the full time clock experience.

### Dashboard Widget

A Time Clock widget appears on your dashboard for quick access:
- Shows your current status
- One-click clock in/out
- Link to full time clock

## How to Clock In

### From the Staff Portal

1. Navigate to your organization's time clock URL
2. Sign in if prompted
3. If you work at multiple attractions, select where you're working
4. Click the **Clock In** button
5. Your session begins and the timer starts

### From the Dashboard

1. Find the Time Clock widget on your dashboard
2. Click the **Clock In** button
3. You'll be clocked in to your primary attraction

## Current Status Display

When clocked in, you'll see:

| Information | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Status | Green "Clocked In" badge |
| Duration | Time elapsed since clock-in (e.g., "2h 30m") |
| Location | The attraction you're working at |
| Since | The time you clocked in |

When clocked out:

- Gray "Not clocked in" message
- Clock In button available

## How to Clock Out

1. Navigate to the time clock (portal or dashboard)
2. Click the **Clock Out** button
3. Your time entry is recorded

### From Staff Time History (Manager View)

When clocking out a staff member from the admin panel:

1. Enter any break time in minutes
2. Add optional notes about the shift
3. Click **Clock Out**

## Attraction Selection

### Single Attraction

If you're only assigned to one attraction:
- That attraction is auto-selected
- Just click Clock In

### Multiple Attractions

If you work at multiple attractions:
- A selection appears before clocking in
- Choose where you're working today
- Your primary attraction is pre-selected

### Primary Attraction

One attraction can be set as your "Primary":
- Automatically selected when clocking in
- Shown with "Primary" label
- Set by your manager in your staff profile

## Break Time

Break time can be recorded when clocking out:
- Enter break minutes (default: 0)
- Breaks are subtracted from total hours
- Add notes if needed

## Time Entry Details

Each time entry records:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Date | The date of the entry |
| Attraction | Where you worked |
| Clock In | When you started |
| Clock Out | When you finished |
| Break | Break time in minutes |
| Total Hours | Calculated work time |
| Status | Pending or Approved |

## Entry Statuses

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Pending | Entry recorded, awaiting approval |
| Approved | Entry approved by manager |

Pending entries are reviewed and approved by managers for payroll.

## Common Scenarios

### Starting Your Shift

1. Open the time clock on your phone
2. Sign in if needed
3. Select your attraction (if multiple)
4. Tap **Clock In**
5. You're on the clock!

### Ending Your Shift

1. Open the time clock
2. Verify your elapsed time
3. Tap **Clock Out**
4. Your hours are recorded

### Switching Attractions Mid-Shift

If you need to move to a different attraction:
1. Clock out of current attraction
2. Clock back in at new attraction
3. This creates separate time entries

## Permissions

| Action | Who Can Do It |
|--------|---------------|
| Clock self in/out | All staff members |
| Clock others in/out | Owner, Admin, Manager, HR |
| View own entries | All staff members |
| Approve entries | Owner, Admin, Manager, HR |

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Clock In

**"No attractions assigned"**
- You need to be assigned to at least one attraction
- Contact your manager to add you to an attraction

**"Not a staff member"**
- Your staff profile may not be set up
- Contact your manager

### Already Clocked In

- You can't clock in if already clocked in
- Clock out first, then clock in again

### Forgot to Clock Out

- Contact your manager
- They can close out your entry with the correct time
- Time entries can be corrected in the admin panel

### Wrong Attraction

- Clock out immediately
- Clock back in with correct attraction
- Or contact manager to correct the entry

## Best Practices

### Accuracy

- Clock in when you arrive, not before
- Clock out when you leave, not after
- Select the correct attraction

### Breaks

- If taking an unpaid break, record it at clock out
- Be honest about break duration

### Device

- Bookmark the time clock URL on your phone
- Enable notifications for shift reminders
- Keep your phone charged during events

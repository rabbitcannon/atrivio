# Clocking In & Out

Learn how to use the time clock to track your work hours.

![Time Clock](/docs/screenshots/time-clock.png)

## Overview

The time clock allows staff members to clock in and out of their shifts. All time entries are tracked and can be reviewed by managers for payroll processing.

## How to Clock In

1. Navigate to the **Time Clock** page from the sidebar
2. Verify your current status shows "Clocked Out"
3. Click the **Clock In** button
4. Your session will begin and the timer will start

## How to Clock Out

1. Navigate to the **Time Clock** page
2. Click the **Clock Out** button
3. Review your session summary
4. Your hours will be recorded for the shift

## Time Clock Features

### Current Status Display

The time clock shows:
- Your current status (Clocked In / Clocked Out)
- Current session duration
- Today's total hours worked

### Break Management

During your shift, you can:
- Take scheduled breaks
- Clock out for lunch
- Resume your shift after breaks

### Location Verification

If enabled by your organization:
- GPS location may be recorded at clock in/out
- You may need to be within a certain radius of your workplace

## Mobile Access

Access the time clock from your mobile device:
1. Open your browser and navigate to the time clock URL
2. The interface is fully responsive
3. Clock in/out from anywhere (if location restrictions allow)

## Troubleshooting

### Can't clock in?

- Ensure you're scheduled for a shift
- Check if you're within the allowed clock-in window
- Contact your manager if issues persist

### Forgot to clock out?

- Contact your manager to adjust your time entry
- Time entries can be corrected by authorized staff

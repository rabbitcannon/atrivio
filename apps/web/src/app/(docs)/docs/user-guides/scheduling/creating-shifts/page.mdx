# Creating Shifts

Learn how to create and manage staff shifts in the scheduling system.

![Schedule Calendar](/docs/screenshots/schedule-calendar.png)

## Overview

The scheduling system allows managers to create shifts, assign staff, and manage the work schedule for your attraction.

## Creating a New Shift

### From the Calendar View

1. Navigate to **Schedule** in the sidebar
2. Click on a date in the calendar
3. Fill in the shift details:
   - **Start time**: When the shift begins
   - **End time**: When the shift ends
   - **Position**: The role for this shift
   - **Staff member**: Assign to a specific person (optional)
4. Click **Save** to create the shift

### Bulk Shift Creation

Create multiple shifts at once:

1. Click **+ Add Shifts** button
2. Select the date range
3. Choose which days of the week
4. Set the shift times
5. Assign positions and staff
6. Click **Create All**

## Shift Details

Each shift includes:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Date | The date of the shift |
| Start Time | When the shift begins |
| End Time | When the shift ends |
| Position | The role/job for this shift |
| Assigned Staff | Who is working (can be unassigned) |
| Notes | Additional instructions |

## Assigning Staff

### Manual Assignment

1. Click on an unassigned shift
2. Select a staff member from the dropdown
3. The system shows availability conflicts if any
4. Save your assignment

### Auto-Assignment

Let the system suggest assignments:

1. Click **Auto-Assign** for a date range
2. Review the suggestions
3. Approve or modify as needed

## Editing Shifts

### Modify a Shift

1. Click on the shift in the calendar
2. Update any details
3. Click **Save Changes**

### Delete a Shift

1. Click on the shift
2. Click **Delete Shift**
3. Confirm the deletion

## Publishing the Schedule

Shifts are drafts until published:

1. Review all shifts for the period
2. Click **Publish Schedule**
3. Staff will be notified of their assignments

# My Schedule

View your upcoming shifts and personal schedule.

## Overview

The **My Schedule** page is your personal view of assigned shifts and work schedule. This feature helps you:

- See your upcoming shifts at a glance
- View shift details (time, location, role)
- Track your availability
- Plan your week

## Accessing My Schedule

1. Log in to your account
2. Select your organization
3. Click **My Schedule** in the sidebar

## Current Status

This feature is currently under development. When complete, you'll be able to:

### View Upcoming Shifts

See all your assigned shifts with:
- Date and time
- Attraction/location
- Role or position
- Shift notes

### Weekly Calendar

A calendar view showing:
- Your shifts for the week
- Color-coded by attraction
- Quick navigation between weeks

### Shift Details

Click any shift to see:
- Full shift information
- Who else is working
- Special instructions
- Contact information

# Adding Staff Members

Learn how to invite and manage your team in Atrivio.

![Staff List](/docs/screenshots/staff-list.png)

## Inviting New Staff

To add a new team member:

1. Go to **Staff** in the sidebar
2. Click **Invite Staff** button
3. Enter their information:
   - **Email address** (required)
   - **First name** (required)
   - **Last name** (required)
   - **Role** (required)
   - **Phone number** (optional)
4. Click **Send Invitation**

The person will receive an email with:
- A link to create their account
- Instructions for getting started
- Your organization name

## Choosing the Right Role

Select a role based on their responsibilities:

| Role | Best For | Key Permissions |
|------|----------|-----------------|
| **Admin** | General managers | Full access except deletion |
| **Manager** | Shift supervisors | Schedules, check-in, reports |
| **HR** | Human resources | Staff profiles, time tracking |
| **Finance** | Accountants | Reports, payouts, refunds |
| **Box Office** | Ticket sellers | Sales, check-in, basic reports |
| **Actor** | Performers | Clock in/out, view schedule |
| **Scanner** | Door staff | Check-in only |

## Bulk Invitations

To invite multiple people at once:

1. Click **Bulk Import** on the Staff page
2. Download the CSV template
3. Fill in the spreadsheet with staff details
4. Upload the completed file
5. Review and confirm the invitations

## Pending Invitations

Track invitation status in the **Pending** tab:

- **Sent** - Email delivered, awaiting response
- **Opened** - Recipient clicked the link
- **Expired** - Invitation expired (7 days)

You can:
- **Resend** an invitation
- **Cancel** a pending invitation
- **Copy link** to share directly

## Managing Existing Staff

Once staff members join, you can:

### Edit Profile
1. Click on a staff member's name
2. Update their information
3. Click **Save Changes**

### Change Role
1. Go to the staff member's profile
2. Click **Change Role**
3. Select the new role
4. Confirm the change

### Deactivate Account
If someone leaves:
1. Go to their profile
2. Click **Deactivate**
3. Confirm

Deactivated accounts can be reactivated later.

## Staff Profiles

Each staff member has a profile showing:

- Contact information
- Role and permissions
- Time clock history
- Scheduled shifts
- Notes and documents

## Tips for Success

> **Assign appropriate roles** - Start with lower permissions and upgrade as needed.

> **Use real email addresses** - Ensure staff can receive notifications and reset passwords.

> **Keep profiles updated** - Accurate contact info helps with scheduling and emergencies.

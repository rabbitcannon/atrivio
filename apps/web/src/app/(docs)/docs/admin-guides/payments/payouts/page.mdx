# Payouts

Manage how and when you receive your earnings.

## Overview

Payouts transfer your ticket sales revenue to your bank account. Stripe handles payouts based on your configured schedule and settings.

## Payout Schedule

### Automatic Payouts

Configure when funds are transferred:

| Schedule | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Daily | Every business day |
| Weekly | Once per week |
| Monthly | Once per month |

### Payout Timing

After a sale is made:
1. Payment is processed
2. Funds held for standard period (usually 2 days)
3. Payout initiated on schedule
4. Funds arrive in bank (1-2 business days)

## Bank Account

### Linked Account

View your connected bank account:
- Bank name
- Account type
- Last 4 digits

### Update Bank Account

1. Go to **Payments** → **Payouts**
2. Click **Update Bank Account**
3. Enter new account details
4. Verify with micro-deposits (if required)

## Viewing Payouts

### Payout History

See all past payouts:
- Payout date
- Amount

- Bank account

### Payout Details

Click a payout to view:
- Transactions included
- Fees deducted
- Net amount

## Payout Breakdown

### Revenue Components

Each payout includes:

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Gross Sales | Total ticket sales |
| Stripe Fees | Payment processing (2.9% + 30¢) |
| Platform Fees | Atrivio fees |
| Refunds | Returned funds |
| Net Payout | Amount transferred |

### Fee Structure

Standard fees:
- **Stripe**: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- **Atrivio**: Based on your plan

## Manual Payouts

If you prefer manual control:

1. Disable automatic payouts in Stripe
2. Request payouts when needed
3. Monitor balance manually

## Payout Issues

### Pending Payouts

If payout is pending:
- Check account verification status
- Verify bank account is valid
- Review any Stripe alerts

### Failed Payouts

If a payout fails:
1. Check bank account details
2. Ensure account accepts transfers
3. Contact your bank if needed
4. Retry payout

### Balance on Hold

Funds may be held for:
- New accounts
- High-risk transactions
- Dispute reserves

## Tax Information

### Tax Reporting

Stripe provides:
- 1099-K forms (if applicable)
- Transaction reports
- Fee summaries

### Record Keeping

Download reports for:
- Accounting

## Best Practices

1. **Keep bank details updated**: Prevent payout failures
2. **Monitor regularly**: Check payout status weekly
3. **Plan for timing**: Account for payout delays
4. **Track fees**: Understand your cost structure
5. **Save records**: Keep payout reports

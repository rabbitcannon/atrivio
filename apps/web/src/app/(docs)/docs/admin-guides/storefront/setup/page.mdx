# Storefront Setup

Configure your public-facing ticket storefront.

![Storefront Settings](/docs/screenshots/storefront.png)

## Overview

The storefront is your public ticket sales page where customers can browse and purchase tickets. Customize it to match your brand and optimize for conversions.

## Getting Started

### Enable Storefront

1. Navigate to **Storefront** in the sidebar
2. Toggle **Enable Storefront** on
3. Your storefront is now accessible

### Storefront URL

Your storefront is available at:
```
https://[your-slug].atrivio.io
```

Or with a custom domain (Enterprise).

### Theme

Choose a theme style:
- **Light**: Clean, bright design
- **Dark**: Dramatic, immersive feel
- **Custom**: Match your brand

### Colors

Customize your color palette:
- Primary color (buttons, links)
- Secondary color (accents)
- Background color
- Text color

### Images

Add visual elements:
- **Hero Image**: Large banner at top
- **Background**: Page background
- **Gallery**: Attraction photos

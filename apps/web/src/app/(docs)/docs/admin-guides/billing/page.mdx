# Billing & Subscription

Manage your organization's subscription plan and billing information.

## Overview

The **Billing & Subscription** page allows organization owners and admins to:

- View current subscription plan and usage
- Compare available plans
- Upgrade or change subscription tiers
- Monitor resource usage against plan limits

## Accessing Billing

1. Log in to your account
2. Select your organization
3. Go to **Settings** in the sidebar
4. Click **Billing** (under Organization settings)

**Note:** Only users with **Owner** or **Admin** roles can access billing settings.

## Current Plan Overview

![Billing Page Overview](/docs/billing-page.png)

At the top of the page, you'll see your current subscription status:

### Plan Status Card

- **Plan Name** - Your current tier (Free, Pro, or Enterprise)
- **Status Badge** - Shows "Active" when subscription is current
- **Monthly Price** - Your current billing amount

### Usage Progress Bars

Monitor your resource consumption:

| Resource | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Attractions** | Number of attractions created vs. plan limit |
| **Staff Members** | Active staff profiles vs. plan limit |
| **Custom Domains** | Configured domains vs. plan limit |

Progress bars show:
- **Green**: Well within limits
- **Yellow**: Approaching limit (>75%)
- **Red**: At or near limit (>90%)

Three subscription tiers are available:

## Upgrading Your Plan

To upgrade to a higher tier:

1. Review the **Available Plans** section
2. Click **Upgrade to [Plan Name]** on the desired plan
3. You'll be redirected to Stripe's secure checkout
4. Complete payment with your card details
5. Your new plan activates immediately

### What Happens on Upgrade

- New features unlock instantly
- Resource limits increase immediately
- Billing cycle starts from upgrade date
- Prorated charges for partial months

## Downgrading

To downgrade to a lower tier:

1. Contact support before downgrading
2. Ensure you're within the new plan's limits
3. Excess resources will need to be removed or archived

**Warning:** Downgrading may disable features and restrict access to data that exceeds new plan limits.

## Billing FAQ

### When am I charged?

Subscriptions are billed monthly on the anniversary of your signup date.

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

### What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards through Stripe:
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover

### How do I update my payment method?

Click **Manage Billing** to access the Stripe customer portal where you can update payment details.

### What happens if my payment fails?

- You'll receive an email notification
- A 7-day grace period is provided
- After grace period, account reverts to Free tier
- No data is deleted, but features are restricted

### Can I get a receipt?

Yes, Stripe automatically sends receipts for each payment. Access past invoices through the Stripe customer portal.

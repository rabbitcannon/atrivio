# Account Setup

Learn how to create and configure your Atrivio account.

![Register](/docs/screenshots/register.png)

## Creating your account

1. Visit [atrivio.io/register](https://atrivio.io/register)
2. Enter your details:
   - **Email address** - Use your business email for easier collaboration
   - **Password** - At least 8 characters with a mix of letters and numbers
   - **First and last name** - How you'll appear to your team
3. Click **Create Account**
4. Check your email for a verification link

## Email verification

![Login](/docs/screenshots/login.png)

You'll receive an email within a few minutes with a verification link. Click it to activate your account.

> **Didn't receive the email?** Check your spam folder, or click "Resend verification email" on the login page.

## Setting up your profile

Once verified, complete your profile:

1. Click your avatar in the top-right corner
2. Select **Profile Settings**
3. Add a profile photo (optional but recommended)
4. Set your notification preferences

# Setting Up Your First Organization

Organizations are the foundation of Atrivio. They contain all your attractions, staff, tickets, and settings.

## What is an organization?

An organization represents your business. You might have:

- **One organization** if you run a single haunted house
- **Multiple organizations** if you manage separate venues or brands

Each organization has its own:
- Staff members and roles
- Attractions and ticket types
- Payment settings and payouts
- Branding and storefront

## Creating your organization

1. After logging in, click **Create Organization**
2. Fill in the required information:

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Name** | Your business name | Nightmare Manor |
| **Slug** | URL-friendly identifier | nightmare-manor |
| **Timezone** | Your local timezone | America/New_York |

3. Click **Create Organization**

## Organization settings

After creation, configure your organization:

### Basic Information
- **Logo** - Displayed in the dashboard and storefront
- **Description** - Brief overview of your business
- **Contact email** - For customer inquiries

### Business Details
- **Address** - Your physical location
- **Phone number** - Customer support line
- **Website** - Link to your main website

### Branding
- **Primary color** - Used throughout your storefront
- **Accent color** - For highlights and buttons

## Organization roles

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full access, can delete organization |
| **Admin** | Full access, cannot delete organization |
| **Manager** | Manage operations, staff, schedules |
| **HR** | Manage staff profiles and time tracking |
| **Finance** | View reports and manage payments |

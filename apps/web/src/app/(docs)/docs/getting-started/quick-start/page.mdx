# Quick Start Guide

Get your Atrivio account set up and ready to manage your attraction in under 10 minutes.

## What you'll accomplish

By the end of this guide, you'll have:

- Created your Atrivio account
- Set up your first organization
- Added your first attraction
- Invited a team member

## Step 1: Create your account

1. Go to [atrivio.io](https://atrivio.io) and click **Get Started**
2. Enter your email address and create a password
3. Verify your email by clicking the link we send you

![Account creation screen](/docs/screenshots/register.png)

> **Tip:** Use your business email for easier team collaboration later.

## Step 2: Create your organization

After signing in, you'll be prompted to create your first organization.

1. Enter your **organization name** (e.g., "Nightmare Manor")
2. Add a **slug** for your URL (e.g., "nightmare-manor")
3. Select your **timezone**
4. Click **Create Organization**

Your organization is like your business account - it contains all your attractions, staff, tickets, and settings.

## Step 3: Add your first attraction

Now let's add an attraction to your organization:

1. Click **Attractions** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Attraction**
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Name:** Your attraction's name
   - **Type:** Haunted house, escape room, maze, etc.
   - **Capacity:** Maximum guests per session
4. Click **Create Attraction**

## Step 4: Invite your team

You can start inviting staff members right away:

1. Go to **Staff** in the sidebar
2. Click **Invite Staff**
3. Enter their email address
4. Select their role:
   - **Admin** - Full access to everything
   - **Manager** - Can manage day-to-day operations
   - **Actor** - Can clock in/out and view schedules
   - **Box Office** - Can sell tickets and check in guests
5. Click **Send Invitation**

They'll receive an email with instructions to join your organization.

## What's next?

- [Create shift schedules](/docs/user-guides/scheduling/creating-shifts) for your team
